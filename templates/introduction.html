The prices of new cars in the industry are fixed by the manufacturer with some additional costs incurred by the Government in the form of taxes. So customers buying a new car can be assured of the money they invest to be worthy. Predicting the prices of used cars is an interesting and much-needed problem to be addressed. Customers can be widely exploited by fixing unrealistic prices for the used cars and many falls into this trap.
Why customers want to used cars reasons are: Save money and they can upgrade his segment, no cheating here now get certified used cars with warranty, Slower depreciation means they lose less money, Insurance is cheaper, and they need not pay additional charges.
The used car market in India has been the center of attention in the slow growing automotive industry in India.

The project will be developed using Django as a web framework to make website dynamic. Python as backend. MySQL will be used as database for storing the data. Html, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript for the GUI, and frontend. The model will be trained with used car dataset from UCI repository. The project will be consisting of Machine Learning algorithm like Linear Regression, Random forest regression for prediction.
The aim of the project is to use Machine Learning algorithm to develop models for predicting used car price.
